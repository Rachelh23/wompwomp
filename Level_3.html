Rock Paper Scissors -- But with Pokemon?

OVERVIEW:XRPS-Pokemon is a spin on the classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors. Instead of using the standard, boring weapons, we'll be using Pokemon! Instead of rock, paper, and scissors, we'll be using fire, water, and grass type Pokemon. If you beat your opponent -- or vice versa, you get to capture the Pokemon they used for that round and add it to your party.

OBJECTIVE:Capture all of your opponent's Pokemon before you lose all of yours (Oh no!!)

DISCLAIMER: Gen 8 and 9 are not real to me, the franchise stopped at 7.
